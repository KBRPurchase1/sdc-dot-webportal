Welcome to the Secure Data Commons Platform (SDC), a sandbox environment in AWS managed by U.S. Department of transportation. SDC platform provides a collaborative environment for traffic engineer, researchers, data scientists and all those who are interested in carrying out research and analysis on different datasets related to traffic, weather, crash etc.

Ready to Get Started? Follow the steps below or directly jump to any of the sections that you wish to access. SDC Users will have the following access in the platform:

Curated and published datasets Check out the list of currently available datasets (curated, published), data dictionaries owned by you and others
Access to workstations with programming tools Learn more about how you can bring your algorithms and code to the SDC platform
Publish derived datasets and analysis Learn more about how you can publish your analysis and research
Access to workstations with programming tools Access the workstations assigned to you, if you have requested as part of the registration process
